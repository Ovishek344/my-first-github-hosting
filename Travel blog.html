Some delicious popular food of SYLHET

The most famous food in Sylhet is Beef with Satkora, a dish that blends beef with the unique tangy flavor of the Satkora citrus fruit. Other popular dishes include Hawa Biryani, Chungapora, and various dried fish recipes.
Here's a more detailed look at Sylheti cuisine:

  • Beef with Satkora:

    This dish is a hallmark of Sylheti cuisine, featuring tender beef cooked with the local citrus fruit, Satkora, which imparts a distinctive tangy and savory taste.

  • Hawa Biryani:

    A unique biryani dish, known for its flavorful rice and aromatic spices.

  • Chungapora:

    A traditional method of cooking rice, where it is roasted in a bamboo tube over charcoal.

  • Dried Fish Recipes:

    Sylhet is also known for its variety of dried fish preparations.

  • Pitha:

    Traditional rice cakes, representing Sylheti baking artistry.

  • Other popular dishes:

    Mughlai Porota, Pilau, Korma, Biriyani, Kofta, and desserts like Zardah, Firni, and Kheer are also enjoyed in Sylhet.
